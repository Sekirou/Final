CODE1230 Urban Data



Assignment Two

The purpose of this website is to present Australian Bureau of Statistic 2016 Census data as visualisations on graphical maps and understand their data in relation to individual backgrounds. The main Census Data being investigated is G14 (Religious Affiliation by Sex). Data Analysis is completed on the data as well as visualisations based on the different religions versus their dominant zones and the effect of ancestry on individual tendency to follow a certain religion.
