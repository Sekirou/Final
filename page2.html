Religious Affiliation vs. Christian Population





The three dimensional map on the left visually portrays the differing religions living within the vicinity of Sydney, NSW. By examining the legend below, the map shows the different religions dominant in each suburb through colour. The height in this map shows the number of Christians living in the same suburb. As shown on the map, Christians are condensed around the CBD and slowly dissipates outwards away from the CBD. This can imply that wealth may be a factor as to where religions reside as well as space available for land use.
